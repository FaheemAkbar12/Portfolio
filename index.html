About Me

Computer Science Student | Data Science, AI & ML Enthusiast

I am a passionate Computer Science undergraduate at Sukkur IBA University with a strong interest in software and web development. I enjoy building practical, user-focused applications and have hands-on experience in full-stack development, as well as integrating AI-driven solutions into real-world projects.

Beyond technical skills, I actively contribute to the tech community as a student executive member, mentoring peers and organizing initiatives that promote learning and collaboration. I thrive in fast-paced environments and enjoy solving complex problems through creative and structured thinking. I am continuously learning and exploring new technologies, with the goal of growing into a skilled software developer who builds impactful digital solutions.
